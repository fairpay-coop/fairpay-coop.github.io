The Fair Payments Cooperative was founded by Joseph Elliott in June 2015.  If you share our values and interested in joining or collaborating our organization, please contact us.

Joseph Elliott

 
 

Joseph is software architect and social entrepreneur.  As a veteran of the venture capital driven San Francisco Bay Area tech scene he passionately believes that there is a better way to structure enterprises to best serve customers, workers, and society.  He has found great inspiration from the books Reinventing Organizations by Frederic Laloux and Sacred Economics by Charles Eisenstein.

He is saddened by the unnecessary level of transaction fees typically paid out by community serving organizations.  The desire to help the organizations that he cares about to navigate the convoluted world of payment processing is what inspired him to found the Fair Payments Cooperative.
